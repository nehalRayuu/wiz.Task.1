FAQ

Have a question ? We are here to help.

Our customer support is available Monday to Friday: 8am-8:30pm.

Average answer time: 24h
How do you calculate your shipping and handling charges?

We’re dedicated to offering the best shipping methods available to ensure that your order reaches you quickly and safely. Shipping rates will vary, based on the specified delivery method, shipping address and total price of the merchandise in your cart.

What if I received damaged or incorrect merchandise?

In the event that you receive defective, incorrect or incomplete merchandise, please contact us right away so we can make it right. For faster assistance with a return or exchange claim, please email us with your order number, contact information.

What if my order is incomplete?

You're unable to add on further items to an order after purchase. If anything was missed, you'll need to contact us and we can cancel the order for you. You will then need to place a fresh order.

Any question?

You can contact us through our contact page! We will be happy to assist you.
